### Théorème de Shannon
$C = B_{p} \cdot lb\left(1+\frac{S}{B}\right) =  B_{p} \cdot lb\left(1+SNR\right)$

Wifi 8 = 320MHz, SNR = 12

$C = 320 \cdot 10^{9} \cdot lb\left(13\right) = 1184\ \large{Mbits/s} \approx 1\ \large{Gbits/s}$
